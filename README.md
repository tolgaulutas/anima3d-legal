# Privacy Policy — ANIMA 3D

Last updated: May 5, 2025

## 1. Information We Collect
ANIMA 3D collects your email address when you create an account. 
We do not collect any other personal information.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
Your email is used solely for authentication purposes (login, 
password reset). We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase (Google)** — Authentication and database
- **RevenueCat** — Subscription management
- **DigitalOcean** — 3D model storage

## 4. Data Storage
Your account data is stored securely on Firebase servers. 
Downloaded 3D models are stored locally on your device and 
can be deleted at any time from the Profile screen.

## 5. Children's Privacy
ANIMA 3D is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect 
personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
